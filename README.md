# Physical Therapy Clinic Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes 100,000 physical therapy patient visits to evaluate clinic performance, treatment effectiveness, therapist productivity, patient outcomes, insurance reimbursement trends, and revenue generation. The goal is to transform raw healthcare operational data into actionable business insights using Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI.

---

## Business Problem

Physical therapy clinics generate large volumes of patient, treatment, and billing data. Without proper analysis, clinic managers may struggle to identify:

* High-performing treatments
* Revenue-driving services
* Patient outcome trends
* Therapist productivity
* Insurance reimbursement patterns
* Opportunities to improve operational efficiency

This project provides a data-driven solution for monitoring clinical and financial performance.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains **100,000 patient visit records** and includes:

* Patient demographics
* Diagnoses
* Treatment types
* Therapist information
* Visit dates
* Session duration
* Pain scores
* Improvement percentages
* Insurance providers
* Revenue and payment details
* Patient satisfaction scores
* Clinic locations

## Key Business Insights

* Generated over **$23.37M** in total revenue.
* Average patient improvement reached **49.8%**.
* Average patient satisfaction score was **4.08/5**.
* **Balance Training** generated the highest treatment revenue.
* **Fort Worth - Cultural District** was the top-performing clinic location.
* **Stroke Rehabilitation** was the most common diagnosis.
* Higher improvement percentages were associated with increased patient satisfaction.
* Revenue concentration among top treatments highlights opportunities for service expansion.

## Business Recommendations

### Expand High-Performing Services

Increase marketing and scheduling capacity for treatments generating the highest revenue.

### Improve Outcome Tracking

Monitor treatment effectiveness regularly to identify best-performing rehabilitation programs.

### Optimize Insurance Partnerships

Analyze reimbursement patterns and strengthen relationships with high-value insurance providers.

### Replicate Successful Clinic Practices

Benchmark top-performing clinic locations and implement best practices across all facilities.

### Enhance Patient Experience

Continue focusing on treatments that improve both patient outcomes and satisfaction scores.
